# Community Services

| | |
| --- | --- |
| **Primary section** | **Section 6.8 — Evaluation of Community Services** |
| **Evaluation basis** | <ul><li>Training, engagement, support or capacity-building activity records</li><li>Participant registrations and attendance evidence</li><li>Questionnaires, feedback forms or user-study outputs</li><li>Community engagement evidence</li><li>Follow-up or reuse evidence where available</li></ul> |
| **Core dimensions / criteria** | <ul><li>Reach</li><li>Participant experience</li></ul> |
| **Main instruments** | <ul><li>Registration and attendance records</li><li>Participant questionnaires</li><li>Training or event feedback forms</li><li>Engagement analytics where appropriate</li><li>Follow-up surveys or interviews where appropriate</li><li>Pilot or activity evaluation reports</li></ul> |
| **Expected output** | <ul><li>Evidence on reach and participant experience</li><li>Indicators on participation, stakeholder groups and engagement</li><li>Feedback on relevance, usefulness and perceived value</li><li>Recommendations for improving accessibility, delivery or community relevance</li></ul> |
| **What resource providers should prepare** | <ul><li>Description of the community service or activity</li><li>Target audience and objectives</li><li>Registration and attendance data</li><li>Feedback forms or questionnaire results</li><li>Materials, documentation or outputs from the activity</li><li>Evidence of follow-up, reuse or continued engagement where available</li></ul> |
