# AI-enabled Resources

| | |
| --- | --- |
| **Primary section** | **Section 6.9 — Evaluation of AI-enabled resources** |
| **Evaluation basis** | <ul><li>Description of the AI-enabled functionality</li><li>Intended use and use context</li><li>Risk-related documentation</li><li>Transparency and user-information materials</li><li>Performance, reliability or robustness evidence where available</li><li>Human oversight and accountability information</li><li>Pilot or user feedback where relevant</li></ul> |
| **Core dimensions / criteria** | <ul><li>Risk classification and regulatory relevance</li><li>Transparency and explainability</li><li>Human oversight and accountability</li><li>Performance reliability and robustness</li></ul> |
| **Main instruments** | <ul><li>AI risk self-assessment</li><li>Regulatory relevance screening</li><li>Documentation review</li><li>Transparency and explainability review</li><li>Performance, reliability and robustness assessment</li><li>Human oversight and accountability review</li><li>Pilot-based or user-based evaluation where relevant</li></ul> |
| **Expected output** | <ul><li>Evidence on AI-related risks and regulatory relevance</li><li>Assessment of transparency, explainability, oversight and accountability</li><li>Evidence on performance reliability and robustness where available</li><li>Identification of limitations, safeguards or improvement needs</li><li>Input for later monitoring and governance of AI-enabled resources</li></ul> |
| **What resource providers should prepare** | <ul><li>Description of the AI-enabled component or functionality</li><li>Intended users, use cases and operating context</li><li>Available risk classification or risk assessment evidence</li><li>Documentation explaining how the AI-enabled resource works at an appropriate level</li><li>Performance, reliability or robustness evidence where available</li><li>Information on human oversight, accountability and escalation procedures</li><li>Known limitations and safeguards</li></ul> |
