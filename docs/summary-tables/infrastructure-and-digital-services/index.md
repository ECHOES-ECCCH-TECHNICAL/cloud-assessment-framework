# Infrastructure and Digital Services

| | |
| --- | --- |
| **Primary section** | **Section 6.7 — Evaluation of Infrastructure and Digital Services** |
| **Evaluation basis** | <ul><li>Service or infrastructure description</li><li>Technical and operational documentation</li><li>Monitoring and operational evidence</li><li>Security and access-control information</li><li>Incident, availability or reliability evidence where available</li><li>User-facing evidence where the service is directly used by end users</li></ul> |
| **Core dimensions / criteria** | <ul><li>Reliability</li><li>Security</li></ul> |
| **Main instruments** | <ul><li>Operational monitoring</li><li>Service availability and reliability checks</li><li>Security review</li><li>Incident and recovery evidence review</li><li>Documentation review</li><li>User feedback or pilot evaluation where relevant</li></ul> |
| **Expected output** | <ul><li>Evidence on reliability and security of the service or infrastructure component</li><li>Identification of operational risks, security issues or monitoring gaps</li><li>Evidence supporting service quality review</li><li>Recommendations for operational improvement or further monitoring</li></ul> |
| **What resource providers should prepare** | <ul><li>Description of the service or infrastructure component</li><li>Technical and operational documentation</li><li>Available monitoring or availability evidence</li><li>Security and access-control documentation</li><li>Known incidents, limitations or operational risks</li><li>Relevant user feedback or service-support evidence where available</li></ul> |
